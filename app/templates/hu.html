During my junior year at VT, I participated in under-graduate reaserch at the Hume Center for National Security and Technology.

The research involved trying to predict user behavior by looking at packet inter-arrival time. Since we only looked at packet inter-arrival time there is no reason to decrypt the packets. Avoiding decrypting packets means people get less frustrated with regards to privacy.

We found a clear distinction between packet inter-arrival time of audio and video streaming; however, more research is needed to predict other activities.
