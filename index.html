I am currently an intern at Rackspace (Summer 2017). I am working under Mat Grove on the Email Anti-Abuse team. This team is responsible for detecting and stoping bad guys from abusing the Rackspace email system (spaming, phishing, etc). I am currently building a new postmaster site for the team. Click here to see my Hume Center work.

We were trying to predict user behavior by looking only at packet inter-arrival time. Because we only look at the packet inter-arrival time, there is no reason to decreypt the packets. This makes it one step easier and people get less angry when you don't decrypt their information. We only looked at one user activity at a time (web browsing and streaming audio or video). We found a clear distinction between audio and video streaming; however, web browsing looked like a combo of both.
