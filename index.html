As an intern at Rackspace, I will be working on Mat Grove's Email Anti-Abuse team. The team is responsible for detecting and stopping bad guys from abusing the Rackspace email system (spamming, phishing, etc). Currently, I am building a new postmaster site for the team. This site will be used by other postmasters in order to obtain useful information such as their IP reputation, connecting IPs, explain why they can't send or receive email from Rackspace, and why their mailbox might have been shut down. Basically, a one stop shop for information postmasters might need from Rackspace. While at Rackspace I have become familiar with Docker, Ansible, Vagrant, uWSGI, and Flask.

Click here to see my Hume Center work.

The research involved trying to predict user behavior by looking at packet inter-arrival time. Since we only looked at packet inter-arrival time there is no reason to decrypt the packets. Avoiding decrypting packets means people get less frustrated with regards to privacy. We found a clear distinction between audio and video streaming; however, web browsing looked like a combo of both.
